# Product Overview

NeuraForge is an AI-native research operating system that enables seamless collaboration between human researchers and specialized AI agents to accelerate scientific discovery.

## Core Value Proposition

Transform how humanity conducts research by orchestrating domain-specialized AI agents (math, coding, physics, biology, policy) alongside human researchers to tackle complex challenges in medicine, climate, AI safety, and beyond.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Agent Research Orchestration**: Domain-specialized AI agents working in parallel with persistent knowledge graphs
- **Semantic Research Workspace**: Interactive canvases with graphs, equations, and AI commentary
- **Collaborative Agent-Human Papers**: AI co-writes research papers with automated citation and verification
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Socket.io-powered collaborative research sessions
- **Lab Notebook Mode**: Version-controlled experiments with agent-generated summaries

## Target Users

Researchers, scientists, and research teams working on complex problems requiring AI-augmented discovery and collaboration.
