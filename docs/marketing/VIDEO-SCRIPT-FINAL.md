## FILMING INSTRUCTIONS

### What to Record:

1. **You on camera (3 segments):**
   - Introduction (Scene 1)
   - Impact and themes (Scene 9)
   - Next steps and closing (Scenes 10-11)
   
   Total on-camera time: About 2 minutes

2. **Screen recording (1 segment):**
   - Just your dashboard/homepage
   - Navigate around smoothly
   - Show any interactive elements
   - Don't click into unfinished pages
   
   Total screen time: About 2 minutes

### Camera Setup:

- Use your phone or laptop camera
- Eye level, not looking down
- Good lighting (window light or desk lamp)
- Clean background
- Sit comfortably

### Screen Recording Setup:

- Record at 1080p resolution
- Use OBS Studio, QuickTime, or Windows Game Bar
- Close unnecessary tabs/windows
- Hide desktop icons if recording full screen
- Smooth, slow mouse movements

### Audio:

- Use earbuds with mic or laptop mic
- Get close to the microphone (6-8 inches)
- Quiet room, close doors
- Turn off fans and AC if possible
- Speak clearly and at steady pace

### Practice:

- Read the script out loud 3-5 times
- Time yourself (aim for 4-4.5 minutes)
- Practice smooth transitions
- Get comfortable with the words

### Step 1: Import Footage (5 minutes)

- Adjust audio levels

### Step 5: Add Text (10 minutes)

### Step 6: Final Review (10 minutes)
- Watch full video 2-3 times
- Check audio levels (voice should be clear)

- Fix any issues

- Resolution: 1080p

- Quality: High
